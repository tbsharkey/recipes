Peanut Butter and Mayonnaise Sandwich

Enjoy this sandwich as a quick and easy lunch or snack. The combination of flavors might surprise you!

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread (white, wheat, or your preferred type)
  • 2 tablespoons of peanut butter (smooth or chunky)
  • 1 tablespoon of mayonnaise
  • Optional add-ins:
    • Thinly sliced banana
    • Thinly sliced apple
    • Thinly sliced cucumber
    • Lettuce
    • Bacon
    • Honey

Instructions:

  1. Lay out the two slices of bread on a clean surface or plate.
  2. Spread 1 tablespoon of peanut butter on one slice of bread, making sure to cover the entire surface.
  3. Spread 1 tablespoon of mayonnaise on the other slice of bread, covering the entire surface.
  4. If desired, add any optional add-ins to enhance the flavor and texture of your sandwich. You can choose from thinly sliced fruits or vegetables, lettuce, bacon, or a drizzle of honey.
  5. Place the two slices of bread together, with the peanut butter and mayonnaise sides facing each other.
  6. Press down gently to help the sandwich stick together.
  7. If desired, you can toast the sandwich in a sandwich press, on a stovetop grill, or in a pan over low heat for a few minutes on each side, until the bread is golden brown and crispy.
  8. Cut the sandwich into halves or quarters and serve.
